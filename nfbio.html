Redesigning a Cinema Website

THE SITE www.nfbio.dk

MY ASSIGNMENT is to offer an idea for a redesign of the site, based on tests and best practises. The project is entirely unsolicited. I have no contract with Nordiske Filmbiografer, and the case is entirely a solo project.

THE TIMEFRAME for this project has been about 2 months, so far.

This project is still in progress, so I have yet to put the material together and present it properly, but this is where it will go.

I am a huge movie dork, so it makes perfect sense that my first real undertaking as a designer would be a cinema website. I have used it myself plenty of times, and I had a hunch that it could be a good project to practise on. My approach, at first, was simply to to perform a test on the existing site and fix the usability flaws that I found. After having done that, some of the things I found pointed towards a confusing site structure. I decided to flex some creative muscle by redesigning the mobile experience of ordering a ticket on their website. When I am done, I will document my work. So far, this is what I have to show you.
