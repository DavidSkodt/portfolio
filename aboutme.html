Hello there! My name is David, and I am a UX designer from Denmark. I found my passion for user-centered design in the spring of 2017. Since then, I have been teaching myself UX practises through various channels, such as through books, articles, personal projects and online courses. My philosophy is one of constant self-improvement and learning-by-doing, which motivates me to improve every day.

I graduated from Aalborg University, with a Master’s degree in Medialogy with a Specialization in Interaction, in 2015. No idea what that is? Don't worry, it is still fairly new, so I feel like I should touch upon it briefly. Medialogy spans a very broad spectrum of courses and projects, all of which are related to digital media, such as video game creation, general programming, UX and more.

Aside from being a true geek when it comes to technology and video games, I voulenteer with the local branch of Save the Children in a project where we visit an asylum centre twice a month to engage the children through play and events. I also enjoy climbing, cooking and conversation.

I am currently situated in Aalborg, Denmark, but I am willing to move if the right opportunity presents itself!
