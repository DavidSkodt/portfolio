Red Barnet Ungdom music video

THE ORGANIZATION Red Barnet Ungdom is an independent youth organisation that promotes children's rights. About 3.000 young people (below 30) work for Red Barnet Ungdom, a large portion of which are voulenteers.

MY ASSIGNMENT was to create a music video by combining a topical rap track produced by another voulenteer with footage taken from one of the bi-monthly excursions to the local asylum center. I am a voulenteer myself and parttake in these excursions, aside from this humble contribution.

THE TIMEFRAME for this project was about two days.

This music video is the result of creative voulenteer work. Another voulenteer had written- and produced a very topical rap song about what we were doing as voulenteers. I, having some experience in editing and directing amateur video, voulenteered to put a video together. The children in the video are kids from the local asylum center that I voulenteer at. I used Sony Vegas Pro to put the video together.
